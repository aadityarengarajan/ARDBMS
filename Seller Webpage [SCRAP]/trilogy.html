The Software Trilogy

'RDBMS'

Artist-Fan Relations

'Relations Data Base Management System', a part of the Vortex Trilogy Software Suite, is a highly private social media which artists can use to personally interact with each fan, assisted by AI of course, as one single artist can not personally connect with all fans, featuring many unique features to get the fans closer to the artist. Listed as follows :

  • Artist Database of Fans.
  • Community Chat, a group chat with all the registered fans and the artist.
  • Professional Profile Page for each fan.
  • Page to donate to artist for artist development powered by BandCamp.
  • Personal Chat between the fan and the artist, optional - artist can disable it if they wish.
  • Exclusive Artist Previews for fans, unlocked by the fans with points - a point system where the fan earns more points with more messages exchanged in the Community Chat and Personal Chat.
  • Ideations / Artist's Ideas/Thoughts can be streamed to fans with no comment, like, or reply feature to increase mystery for teasing fans, and so the artist may not feel judged.
  • Artist's personal message to each fan, may be set by the artist, or AI generated based on the fan's Spotify account - by default, it will be handled by the AI.

'MODUS OPERANDI'

Artist Productivity

An artist management dashboard software for managers managing multiple artists, or record labels, providing lots of features to the artist to boost productivity including - the possibility to host a radio show, an online DAW powered by AudioTool, the ability for artists to draft releases to respective managers, free online mixing and mastering, and much more unique features to boost the artist's productivity.

'SHOP'

Artist Finance

A simplistic free online shop with a unique and attractive pre-built template for the artist, highly customizable by front-end developers with the unique feature to replace the pre-built template with custom HTML code, easy to use, automatic delivery cost calculation, and sales powered by Stripe.

  • Pre-Built Webpages as templates, artist only has to get a Stripe account to connect, and artist has the liberty to personally setup production and delivery, however the online presence and mediation between buyer and seller is managed by us.
  • Easy to make changes to templated webpages by simply uploading custom HTML code without hassle.
  • Given location of production, delivery charges will automatically be calculated for international shipping based on the latest aviation economy, taking into consideration distance, time, fuel costs, transportation costs, etc.
  • Automatic payment using Stripe with buyer's credit card. All the artist has to do is connect Stripe account to Vortex. And all the buyer has to do is enter card number and expiry date, transactions will be easy, simplistic, quick, and taken care of by Stripe.
